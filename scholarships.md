# 🎓 Scholarships for Students Joining College in India

| # | Scholarship Name | Link | Eligibility |
|---|-----------------|------|-------------|
| 1 | **Central Sector Scheme of Scholarships (CSSS)** | [scholarships.gov.in](https://scholarships.gov.in) | Class 12 passed with 80th percentile+, family income ≤ ₹8 LPA, enrolled in regular degree course |
| 2 | **National Scholarship Portal – Post-Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC)** | [scholarships.gov.in](https://scholarships.gov.in) | SC/ST/OBC students, passed Class 10/12, pursuing higher education, income limits vary by state |
| 3 | **Pradhan Mantri Scholarship Scheme (PMSS)** | [scholarships.gov.in](https://scholarships.gov.in) | Wards/widows of ex-servicemen & paramilitary, enrolled in 1st year of professional degree (BE/MBBS/MBA etc.), min 60% in Class 12 |
| 4 | **AICTE Pragati Scholarship (for Girls)** | [aicte-pragati-saksham-gov.in](https://aicte-pragati-saksham-gov.in) | Girl students admitted to 1st year of AICTE-approved Degree/Diploma, family income ≤ ₹8 LPA, only 1 girl per family |
| 5 | **AICTE Saksham Scholarship (for Specially Abled)** | [aicte-pragati-saksham-gov.in](https://aicte-pragati-saksham-gov.in) | Students with 40%+ disability, admitted to 1st year of AICTE-approved Degree/Diploma, family income ≤ ₹8 LPA |
| 6 | **Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY)** | [kvpy.iisc.ac.in](http://kvpy.iisc.ac.in) | Students enrolled in 1st year of UG in Basic Sciences (B.Sc/B.S/B.Stat/B.Math/Int. M.Sc), min 60% in Class 12 (55% for SC/ST/PwD) |
| 7 | **Tata Scholarship (Cornell & India Programs)** | [tatatrusts.org](https://www.tatatrusts.org) | Indian students from low-income families, merit-based, pursuing UG programs; specific criteria per program |
| 8 | **Reliance Foundation Undergraduate Scholarships** | [reliancefoundation.org](https://www.reliancefoundation.org) | 1st year UG students in STEM or Humanities/Liberal Arts, family income ≤ ₹2.5 LPA, min 60% in Class 12 |
| 9 | **Sitaram Jindal Foundation Scholarship** | [sitaramjindalfoundation.org](https://www.sitaramjindalfoundation.org) | Students pursuing Degree/Diploma in any stream, low family income, minimum 55–60% marks in qualifying exam |
| 10 | **Vidyasaarathi Scholarship (various corporates)** | [vidyasaarathi.co.in](https://www.vidyasaarathi.co.in) | Varies by corporate sponsor; generally 1st year UG/Diploma students with income ≤ ₹4–6 LPA and 60%+ in Class 12 |

---

> **Tips to Apply:**
> - Most deadlines fall between **July – October** — apply early after admissions.
> - Keep documents ready: Aadhaar, income certificate, Class 12 marksheet, admission letter, bank passbook.
> - The **National Scholarship Portal (NSP)** at [scholarships.gov.in](https://scholarships.gov.in) is a one-stop portal for most government scholarships.
